What wallets do you recommend?

For ease and flexibility, we recommend Abra, Lumi or BitPay mobile wallets.

Where can I swap cryptocurrencies?

To swap virtually any crypto fast, easy and anonymously, check out Changelly or ShapeShift.

What exchanges do you recommend?

For USA customers, the largest are Coinbase, Kraken or Binance. For international customers, visit CryptoRadar and use the filters to narrow down your search.

What are other P2P exchanges?

For peer-to-peer bank and mobile wallet transfers, we recommend Paxful, LocalBitcoins or Bisq.

Where can I analyze crypto trends?

For real-time visual data charts on the entire crypto market, visit Coin360 or CryptoWatch.

What software does Redeeem use?

Redeeem uses Bcoin, CoinPayments, Civic and a dozen other software programs listed on our Product page.

What browser do you recommend?

We recommend Brave, a faster, more private and secure browser built on Basic Attention Token (BAT). To win disputes, we require the Loom Chrome extension.

How do I convert Amazon to bitcoin?

We recommend Purse.io or iPayYou.io to convert Amazon credits back to bitcoin.
